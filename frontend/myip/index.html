Privacy

This tool stores no results. Displayed data (IP, ASN, country, STUN observations, RTT) are computed on the fly and stay in your browser. Requests to Cloudflare, ipify, RIPE Stat and public STUN servers are sent directly from your browser — your IP is therefore visible to them. No tracking cookies.
